Partnerships

DIA collaborates with academic institutions, alumni networks, and research communities to expand access to high-level scientific exchange.

Building a Network of Academic Solidarity

The Duby Intercollegiate Associateship was founded on a simple conviction: meaningful knowledge should circulate beyond the traditional boundaries of institutions. From its inception, DIA has sought to work alongside schools, universities, alumni communities, and independent researchers who share this commitment.

Our partnerships are not commercial arrangements. They are collaborations grounded in intellectual curiosity, scientific rigor, and the desire to connect motivated students with high-quality research and academic dialogue.

Through these relationships, DIA aims to progressively build an international network linking students and scholars from leading institutions while remaining fully independent and non-profit in spirit.
